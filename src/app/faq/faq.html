Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your visit to Taniti

Essential Information

What type of power outlets does Taniti use?

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States. You won't need any special adapters if you're traveling from the US.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its primary currency. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and most businesses accept major credit cards.

What languages are spoken on Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, especially in tourist areas. However, very little English is spoken in rural areas, particularly by older residents. Learning a few basic local phrases can be helpful for exploring remote areas.

Health & Safety

What medical facilities are available?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees to assist international visitors with their healthcare needs.

How safe is Taniti for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti. However, as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes. Take standard precautions with your valuables and stay aware of your surroundings.

Safety Tips:

  • Keep valuables secure and out of sight.
  • Use hotel safes for important documents.
  • Stay in well-lit, populated areas at night.
  • Be cautious with displays of expensive items.

Alcohol & Entertainment

What are the alcohol service hours?

Alcohol cannot be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM. Plan your evening activities accordingly.

12:00 AM - 9:00 AM No Alcohol Sales

What is the legal drinking age?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 years old, and enforcement is generally not strict. However, visitors should still carry valid identification.

Planning Your Visit

Are there many holidays when attractions might be closed?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on these holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly and check operating schedules in advance.

Tip: Contact attractions and restaurants ahead of time to confirm their holiday schedules during your visit.

Do I need to book experiences in advance?

Most experiences require 24-48 hours notice for booking. Popular activities like helicopter rides and boat charters may need even more advance planning during peak season.

What should I pack for Taniti?

Essential Items:

  • Sunscreen and sun protection.
  • Light, breathable clothing.
  • Comfortable walking shoes.
  • Swimwear and beach gear.
  • Valid identification.

Optional Items:

  • Snorkeling gear (if you have your own).
  • Camera for capturing memories.
  • Light rain jacket for tropical showers.
  • Insect repellent for jungle areas.
