Transportation

Everything you need to know about traveling to and around Taniti.

Getting to Taniti

By Air

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air. Our local airport accommodates small jets and propeller planes.

  • Current aircrafts: Small jets and propeller planes.
  • Future expansion: The airport is being expanded to accommodate larger jets within the next few years.

By Sea

A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week, offering a unique way to arrive at the island.

  • Schedule: One night per week.
  • Location: Yellow Leaf Bay.

Getting Around the Island

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City with reliable daily service.

  • Service hours: 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
  • Frequency: Every day.
  • Coverage: Taniti City.

Private Buses

Private buses serve the rest of the island beyond Taniti City, providing access to all areas.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City for convenient point-to-point transportation.

Rental Cars

Rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport, giving you the freedom to explore at your own pace.

Bicycle Rentals

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors around the island.

Important: Helmets are required by law when cycling.

Walking

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable, making it perfect for exploring on foot.

  • Taniti City: Flat terrain, easy to navigate
  • Merriton Landing area: Popular tourist area, easy to explore on foot

Transportation Tips

Airport Convenience

Rental cars are conveniently located near the airport for easy pickup upon arrival.

Tourist Areas

Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing, which is easy to explore on foot.

City Layout

Taniti City's flat terrain makes it ideal for walking, cycling, or using public transportation.
